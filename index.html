About

Chung-Hay Luk is a User Experience (UX) researcher and designer who approaches her craft with a compassionate heart and scientific mind. She strives to build a better future -- having improved doctor-patient interactions through web interfaces and wearables for Augmedix, a Google Glass-partnered startup; and reimagined brain fitness software in new settings at Posit Science. She also loves side projects ranging from UX research and design to prototyping.
