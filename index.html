About

I'm a User Experience (UX) researcher and designer, and I enjoy solving problems that people don't realize they have. I'm strong at systems thinking, which facilitates understanding of contextual user behavior and creation of thorough design specs. I value qualitative and quantitative data in informing design, given my 8+ years doing psychological research.

Outside of work, I indulge in kawaii things, traveling, and prototyping interactive experiences. Ideally all at once!
