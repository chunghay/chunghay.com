Published Art

  • The computer that can belly dance. (2009) NBC Bay Area News. Narr. Jodie Hernandez [Video: VHS] San Francisco, CA: NBC, 3rd December, PST: 18:00.

Interviewed on the Heartbeat Jenga project, an interactive version of the Jenga board game that promotes calmness, in that the game is played on a platform that shakes with increases in the player’s heart rate. Heart rate was monitored via my handcrafted electronic scarf with surface electrodes at the ends.
