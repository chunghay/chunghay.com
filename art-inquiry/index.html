Requests for Commissioned Art

I’m always delighted to help you realize your project! Commissioned art starts with a meeting (virtual or in person), where we work through the design together. If the project is more involved, I present sketches or prototypes during subsequent meetings until we’re happy with the design. Afterward, I create the piece, accounting for any feedback along the way.

Examples of commissioned work:

  • Wedding cake topper
  • Graphic t-shirts
  • Light-up clothing
