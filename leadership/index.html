Leadership

Recycling Coordinator • Neuroscience Department, University of California, Berkeley • 2006-present

  • Running the plastic and aluminum recycling for much of the neuroscience department

Social Chair • Neuroscience Department, University of California, Berkeley • 2006-2010

  • Founded monthly networking dinners for graduate students in the neuroscience community
  • Conceptualized, organized and hosted the annual formal to bring together alumni and current students

Student Seminar Series Committee Member • Neuroscience Department, University of California, Berkeley • 2006-2009

  • Streamlined the process we used to invite speakers and organize the visit, in addition to hosting speakers during their visits

Society of Women Engineers Treasurer • University of California, Berkeley chapter • 2003-2004

  • Detected deficit spending from past years and restructured operations to cut costs. Notably, worked with corporate relations to move our career fair-and-dinner event to its current and more affordable location

Engineering Student Council Financial Committee Member • University of California, Berkeley • 2004

  • Determined the funding for over 30 engineering student organizations, groups, and competition teams

Society of Women Engineers Junior Solar Sprint Challenge Chair • University of California, Berkeley chapter • 2002-2003

  • Fundraised and organized the local branch of a national competition hosted by the U.S. Department of Energy, where middle-school teams built and raced solar cars

Awarded the Society of Women Engineers Region A Excellent Outreach Program Award (June 2003).
Region A includes northern California, northern Nevada, and Hawaii

Earth Club President • Campolindo High School, Moraga, CA • 2000-2001

  • Represented Earth Club in Earth Team, a San Francisco Bay Area grassroots leadership movement for youths
  • Coordinated with janitors to installed hawk silhouettes on the library windows to avert birds from crashing into them

Earth Club Treasurer • Campolindo High School, Moraga, CA • 1999-2000

  • Wrote and awarded external funding from the Contra Costa Solid Waste Authority’s mini-grant program to start the plastic and aluminum recycling program at the school. One of 11 grants funded
  • Convinced administrators to create a bulletin board to communicate environmental news and issues on campus
